Areas covered

Areas We Cover for Commercial Landscaping in Kent

We provide commercial landscaping services throughout Kent and Medway, including Chatham, Rochester, Gillingham, Maidstone, Aylesford, Tonbridge, Sevenoaks, Dartford, Gravesend, Strood, Rainham, Walderslade, and surrounding areas.

How much does commercial landscaping cost in Kent?
Commercial landscaping costs vary depending on the scope of the project, materials required, and site access. Small projects typically range from £2,000–£8,000. Medium projects from £8,000–£25,000. Larger full-site transformations from £25,000+. We provide free, no-obligation site surveys and detailed written quotations.
Are you insured for commercial landscaping work?
Yes. Roundwood Solutions carries full public liability insurance and all operatives hold relevant certifications. We are BALI-accredited, providing additional assurance of our professional standards.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance after landscaping projects?
Yes. Many of our landscaping clients also take a grounds maintenance contract with us, ensuring their new outdoor spaces are kept in perfect condition year-round.
